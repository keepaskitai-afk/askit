What AI can do —
and what it can't

⏱ 10 min 🧩 Interactive sorter 👥 Real stories

Most people either expect too much from AI or too little. They either think it can do everything — or they've tried it once, it got something wrong, and they wrote it off completely.

The truth is much more useful: AI is incredible at certain things and genuinely bad at others. Once you know which is which, you stop wasting time and start getting real results.

The honest truth
about AI limits

Even people who use AI every day get burned sometimes. Here's what AI genuinely struggles with — not to scare you, but so you know when to double-check.

🕐
It's frozen in time
AI's knowledge has a cutoff date. It doesn't know what happened last week, last month, or even last year. Always verify current facts.
🎭
It sounds confident when it's wrong
AI doesn't say "I'm not sure" the way a human would. It can state incorrect information in a completely convincing tone.
🫀
It doesn't know you
AI has no memory between conversations. It doesn't know your history, your relationships, your tone — unless you tell it.
🔢
It's not a calculator
AI can make basic math errors. For important numbers — finances, dosages, measurements — always verify independently.
💡 The rule that saves you every time

Use AI to do the work, use your brain to check the work. You're the editor — AI is the very fast first drafter.

Where AI
genuinely shines

To balance the picture — here's what AI does that nothing else does as well.

⚡
Instant first drafts
Emails, reports, messages, proposals — AI gets you 80% of the way there in seconds. You just refine and personalise.
🔍
Explaining anything, simply
Ask it to explain a legal document, a medical term, a tax form — and ask it to simplify until it clicks. Infinitely patient.
🧠
Thinking partner at 2am
Brainstorming, stress-testing ideas, playing devil's advocate — AI never gets tired, never judges, always engages.
🌍
Language and translation
Write in any language, translate naturally, adjust tone from casual to formal. Language tasks are where AI consistently excels.
Key takeaway

AI is a powerful tool with real limits. Know both, and you'll use it better than 95% of people who try it.
